Therapy

Judith Fujimura offers flexible scheduling at her office, located at 303 5th Avenue.

Judith's fee is $150 per 60-minute session. Appointments can be scheduled by calling or texting Judith at 917-584-7107, or through her email, Judy@Judycares.com.

Cancellations must be made 24 hours in advance. Clients will be charged $150 for late cancellations or missed appointments. Phone and Skype sessions are also available.

The privacy of your health care information deserves to be protected. Therefore, Judy Fujimura’s practice conforms to the highest standards of confidentiality.

Judy Fujimura is not associated with any insurance carriers. However, an invoice for service can be provided containing all of the information needed to submit an out-of-network claim.

Relationships in all of their diverse forms are valuable contexts for human connecting and growth. Therefore, Judy Fujimura’s practice includes clients with diverse relationship histories; traditional and non-traditional family groups; people of all religious faith traditions or with no faith perspective, as well as people from all ethnic and socioeconomic groups.
